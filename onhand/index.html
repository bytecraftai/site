What it does

  • Type what’s in your fridge, see meals ranked by match
  • An honest match percentage and a list of what you’re missing
  • One-line how-to on every card — no blog stories, no scrolling for the recipe
  • Macros visible: calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber
  • Filter by course, time, method, or preference (high-protein, vegetarian, etc.)
  • Heart your favorites for one-tap access later
  • Recipe library grows quietly in the background — no app updates needed
Private by design. Onhand collects nothing, sends nothing, tracks nothing. Your ingredients, your favorites, your history — all stay on your phone. No servers, no analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs.
