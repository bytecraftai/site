What is AllTaps?

A tiny, cute, no-fuss golf scorecard for Apple Watch. Tap once for each shot, advance to the next hole, and at the end of your round get a beautifully designed scorecard ready to text to yourself or share with friends. That’s the whole pitch.

  • 9 or 18 hole rounds
  • Big tappable counter — no fumbling between swings
  • Speak the course name, or type it
  • Save & resume — your watch dies, your round doesn’t
  • Cute illustrated scorecards generated for each round
  • Share your scorecard image with a tap
  • Browse your full round history

How to get it

Easiest: install right on your watch

  1. Open the App Store on your Apple Watch (the blue “A” icon on the watch).
  2. Search AllTaps.
  3. Tap Get. It’s free and installs in seconds, right on the watch.

Or install from your iPhone

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone, or tap the direct link: apps.apple.com/us/app/alltaps
  2. Tap Get. The iPhone container installs first.
  3. The watch app usually auto-installs within a minute. If it doesn’t, open the Watch app on your iPhone → scroll to Available Apps → tap Install next to AllTaps.

Either way: on your Apple Watch, find the AllTaps icon (coral flag on a teal background) and tap it open. ⛳ No accounts to create, no settings to configure. Tap. Score. Share.

How to use it

Starting a round

  1. Open AllTaps on your watch.
  2. Tap Start a New Round.
  3. Choose 9 Holes or 18 Holes.
  4. (Optional) Tap the course name field and dictate it (mic icon) or type it. Or just hit Skip & Start.

During the round

  • Tap the big number in the middle each time you take a shot.
  • Hit the − button if you mis-tap and need to remove a shot.
  • Use the ‹ and › chevrons to move between holes.
  • On the final hole, tap the checkered flag to finish.
  • Need to step away mid-round? Tap Save & Exit. When you reopen the app, “Resume Round” will be waiting at the top.

Sharing your scorecard

  1. After you finish a round, tap Share Scorecard. AllTaps generates a cute illustrated scorecard image.
  2. Pick where it goes from the share sheet — text it to yourself or a friend via Messages, or send it by Mail.

Wait, that’s it?

Yep. On purpose.

AllTaps is small, fast, and stays out of your way. It lives on your wrist — not on a dashboard you’ll never look at, not in a CRM of your shots, not in a feed asking how you felt about hole 7.

What we deliberately left out

  • No GPS, yardage, or hole maps. Other apps do that beautifully. This isn’t that.
  • No swing analysis. We don’t have an opinion about your swing.
  • No subscriptions or signup. The app you download is the app you get.
  • No leaderboards or social feed. Your group chat works fine. Use that.
  • No notifications. You’ll never get a push from AllTaps telling you to “come back.”
  • No tracking. Every round stays on your wrist. We have no servers.

If you want a Swiss Army knife, the App Store is full of them. We made a tee. ⛳

Quick FAQ

How much does it cost?

Free at launch.

What Apple Watch do I need?

AllTaps works on Apple Watch Series 7 or later, running watchOS 10 or later. Most watches from 2021 onward.

Does it work without my iPhone nearby?

The app itself works offline anywhere your watch is. The only feature that needs your iPhone nearby is Share Scorecard — it hands the scorecard image to your phone’s share sheet.

Will it drain my watch battery?

Nope. AllTaps only does anything when you have it open. No background activity, no sensors, no tracking.

What if I find a bug or have a feature idea?

Email hello@bytecraftco.ai. Real humans read every message.

Will AllTaps come to Android Wear?

Maybe! It’s on the wishlist. Email us if you’d use it on Android — that’s the kind of feedback that pushes it up the priority list.

Can I see all my old rounds?

Yes — tap Previous Rounds on the home screen. (It only appears once you’ve finished at least one round.)
