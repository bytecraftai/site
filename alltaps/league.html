Welcome! You’re here because you’re part of the league (or a very lucky friend), and AllTaps is launching on the App Store. ⛳

AllTaps is a tiny, cute, no-fuss golf scorecard for Apple Watch. Tap once for each shot, advance to the next hole, share the scorecard. That’s the whole pitch.

On the App Store

How to get it 🎉

AllTaps is a free download for Apple Watch. Here’s how to grab it:

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone (the blue “A” icon).
  2. Search AllTaps. Or tap the direct App Store link once it’s live: apps.apple.com/... — coming soon
  3. Tap Get. It’s free. The app installs on your watch automatically — no extra steps.
  4. On your Apple Watch, find the AllTaps icon (coral flag on a teal background) and tap it open. ⛳

No accounts, no signup, no settings to configure. Tap. Tally. Share.

How to use it

Starting a round

  1. Open AllTaps on your watch and tap Start a New Round.
  2. Choose 9 Holes or 18 Holes.
  3. (Optional) Tap the course name field and dictate it (mic icon) or type it — or just hit Skip & Start.

During the round

  • Tap the big number each time you take a shot.
  • Hit the − button if you mis-tap and need to remove a shot.
  • Use the ‹ and › chevrons to move between holes.
  • On the final hole, tap the checkered flag to finish.
  • Need to step away? Tap Save & Exit — “Resume Round” will be waiting when you reopen the app.

Sharing your scorecard

After you finish, tap Share Scorecard. AllTaps generates a cute illustrated scorecard image and hands it to the share sheet — text it to yourself or a friend via Messages, or send it by Mail.

Quick FAQ

Wait, do I need to pay anything?

Nope. AllTaps is free at launch.

What Apple Watch do I need?

AllTaps works on Apple Watch Series 7 or later, running watchOS 10 or later — most watches from 2021 onward.

Does it work without my iPhone nearby?

The app itself works offline anywhere your watch is. The only feature that needs your iPhone nearby is Share Scorecard — it hands the scorecard image to your phone’s share sheet.
