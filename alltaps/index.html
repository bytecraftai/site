Privacy Policy

AllTaps ⛳

By Bytecraft Co.

Last updated: May 6, 2026

The short version: AllTaps collects nothing about you. Everything stays on your Apple Watch. We have no servers, no analytics, no third parties. We can't see your scores even if we wanted to.

What information we collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any personal information.

What stays on your Apple Watch

The following is stored locally on your watch (in UserDefaults) and never leaves your device:

  • Your golf rounds (course names, hole counts, shot totals, and dates)
  • App preferences (the rotating tagline counter)

Uninstalling the app erases all of this. We have no copy.

Sharing scorecards

When you tap “Share Scorecard,” AllTaps hands a scorecard image to Apple’s system share sheet. You choose where it goes (Messages or Mail). AllTaps has no involvement after that.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising or ad tracking identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No location, health, contacts, microphone, or camera access
  • No accounts, sign-ins, or passwords
  • No background data collection

Children

AllTaps is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the “Last updated” date at the top will change too. The current version always lives at this URL.
