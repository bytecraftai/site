Welcome! You’re here because you’re part of the league (or a very lucky friend) and you’re about to be one of the very first humans on Earth with Pin & Tally on your wrist. ⛳

Pin & Tally is a tiny, cute, no-fuss golf scorecard for Apple Watch. Tap once for each shot, advance to the next hole, share the scorecard. That’s the whole pitch.

Here’s how this is going to work — in two phases:

Phase 1 · Now

The Beta 🧪

For the next two weeks, you and a small handful of friends will be the only people in the world with Pin & Tally. Use it for your rounds, abuse it for science, and tell us when something feels weird.

What you’ll need

  • An Apple Watch (Series 7 or later) paired to your iPhone
  • The TestFlight app (free, made by Apple) — install it from the App Store if you don’t have it
  • About 90 seconds and a sense of humor

How to install

  1. Open TestFlight on your iPhone (after installing it from the App Store).
  2. Tap the link Jaime sends you — something like testflight.apple.com/join/XXXXXXXX
  3. Tap Accept, then Install. Pin & Tally appears on your watch within a minute or two.
  4. Open Pin & Tally on your watch from your app grid. Tap a couple buttons. Try not to scream with joy.

What to do during the beta

  • Use it for at least one real round. Anything weird? Tell us.
  • Try the “Text Myself” feature after finishing a round (Settings → enter your phone number first, then tap Text Myself on the scorecard).
  • Share a scorecard with a friend at least once.
  • Save & Exit mid-round and resume later — make sure it actually picks up where you left off.
Bugs? Weird vibes? Wishlist items?
Email hello@bytecraftco.ai with anything — even “the pink could be slightly more peach.” All feedback genuinely helps. Include your watch model and what you were doing when it happened, if you remember.

Beta heads-up

  • It’s a beta. Things may occasionally crash, look funny, or behave oddly. That’s the whole point.
  • Updates roll out fast. When Jaime ships a fix, TestFlight will show a little badge — tap to grab the latest.
  • Your data stays on your watch. Same as the live app — no servers, no tracking. (See the Privacy Policy.)
  • The beta auto-expires. TestFlight builds stop working after 90 days unless we ship a new one. By then, the App Store version will be live.
Phase 2 · ~Two Weeks From Now

The App Store 🎉

Once we’ve had ~2 weeks of you all kicking the tires and we’ve fixed any bugs that turned up, Pin & Tally goes live on the public App Store. From then on, anyone with an Apple Watch can grab it.

How to download once it’s live

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone.
  2. Search Pin & Tally. (Or tap the direct link Jaime will share — apps.apple.com/... — coming soon)
  3. Tap Get. The app installs on your watch automatically.
  4. Already have the beta? You can delete the TestFlight version once the App Store version is on your watch — the App Store version replaces it cleanly.

What changes vs. the beta?

  • The same Pin & Tally you tested, plus any fixes/tweaks from your feedback during the beta.
  • Permanent — won’t expire after 90 days.
  • You’ll get future updates automatically.
  • Show your friends! ⛳

Help spread the word

Once we’re live, the single biggest thing you can do is leave a quick App Store review (even one sentence!). Reviews help other golfers find the app. We’ll send a friendly reminder when the time comes.

Quick FAQ

Wait, do I need to pay anything?

Nope. The beta is free. The App Store version is planned to be free at launch.

Can I share the TestFlight link with friends not in the league?

Yes! The more testers, the better. Send them this page or just the TestFlight invite link.

I lost my round! Why?

It shouldn’t happen, but if it does, please email hello@bytecraftco.ai with what happened — that’s exactly the kind of bug a beta is for.

I have a feature idea.

Send it our way! Pin & Tally is intentionally tiny and we don’t want to bloat it, but we want to know what you’d love to see. Beta feedback shapes what ships at launch.

How do I get help?

Email hello@bytecraftco.ai or peek at the Support page.
